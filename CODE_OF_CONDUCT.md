# Code of Conduct

Project Autopoiesis uses an integrity-first collaboration model.

Participants must:

- Preserve truthful reporting of uncertainty and failure.
- Respect user autonomy and privacy boundaries.
- Keep economic incentives outside memory, truth, and audit logic.
- Avoid deceptive, manipulative, spam, surveillance, or unsafe autonomy work.
- Record assumptions and risks when a design affects spending, privacy, or
  external compute.

The owner may reject contributions that weaken local sovereignty, auditability,
or the non-negotiable constraints in `MASTER_CONTEXT.md`.
