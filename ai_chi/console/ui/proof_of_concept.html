What makes it different

▸Three-state truth. Every claim is + confirmed − rejected or = suspended. Genuine uncertainty is preserved, never guessed.
▸Audits, never acts. The reasoning core produces audited belief and emits provenance-stamped messages — it can't take actions.
▸Hybrid by design. Heavy reasoning on the low-power NPU; embeddings on CPU. Fully offline — no cloud.
▸Out-of-loop self-check. It caught and rejected a faked test in its own pipeline.
