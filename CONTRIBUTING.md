# Contributing

SIGMABUS welcomes implementations, schema fixes, protocol review, and transport
binding experiments.

By submitting a contribution, you agree that your contribution is provided under
the GNU General Public License v3.0 unless you clearly state otherwise.

For safety-impacting changes, include:

- The protocol section affected.
- The failure mode or hazard addressed.
- Compatibility notes for existing v0.1 implementors.
- A validation plan, including edge or offline behavior where relevant.
