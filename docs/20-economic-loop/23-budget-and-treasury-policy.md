# Budget and Treasury Policy

## Purpose

Define how Autopoiesis simulates reserves, burn rate, risk buffers, and
reinvestment.

## Accounts

- Reserve balance.
- Survival floor.
- Compute budget.
- Storage budget.
- Risk reserve.
- Experiment pool.
- Reinvestment pool.

## Hard Defaults

- Real spending is off.
- Treasury writes are simulated.
- Owner approval is required for paid jobs.
- Emergency reserve cannot be spent by autonomous policy.

## Audit

Every budget decision records purpose, estimate, approval status, selected tier,
and actual outcome when available.
