# Bittensor Usefulness Markets

## Candidate Role

Bittensor is a research target for useful-output incentives and decentralized
intelligence markets.

## Integrity Risk

Market incentives can reward outputs that score well without being true,
private, useful, or aligned with the owner. Autopoiesis must treat these signals
as external evidence, not ground truth.

## Phase 0 Decision

Study the incentive model and benchmark structure. Do not make the Cognitive
Matrix dependent on Bittensor rewards or scoring.
