# Akash Compute Procurement

## Candidate Role

Akash may provide rented compute for sanitized batch jobs when local resources
are too slow, unavailable, or cost-inefficient.

## Required Before Use

- Price and availability research.
- Data classification.
- Sanitization plan.
- Owner approval for real spending.
- Result verification plan.
- Rollback or retry policy.

## Refusal Cases

Do not use Akash for private memory, credentials, raw internal logs, or jobs that
cannot be checked after completion.
